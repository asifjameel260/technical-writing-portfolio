# Avatar: Fire and Ash: A Viewer's Guide

This guide helps movie fans understand what to expect from the third installment of James Cameron's franchise. 

In this guide, you will learn:
* Where to watch it online
* About the new fire clans of Pandora

## Availability

Avatar: Fire and Ash is currently available for streaming on [JioHotstar](https://www.jiohotstar.com). 

To watch the movie, you need:
* A valid JioHotstar subscription.
* A stable internet connection.

## The Ash People

The third movie introduces a darker, more aggressive faction of Na'vi known as the "Ash People". 

> **Important Note:** Unlike the previous clans who worshiped nature, this fire clan represents the anger and destructive nature of Pandora.
