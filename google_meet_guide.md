# How to Schedule a Google Meet Video Call

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to schedule a video meeting using Google Meet. 

## Prerequisites

Before you schedule a meeting, ensure you have the following:
* A Google (Gmail) account
* A smartphone/computer
* A web browser like Chrome and stable internet connection

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Open your web browser and navigate to [Google Meet](https://meet.google.com).
2. Click the **New meeting** button on the screen.
3. Select **Create a meeting for later** or **Start an instant meeting** from the dropdown menu.
4. Copy the meeting link you get and share it with friends/colleagues.

## Troubleshooting

If your participants cannot hear or see you during the call, use the following tip:

> **Tip:** Ensure that you have granted Google Meet permission to access your **camera and microphone** in your browser settings. 

This concludes the user guide.
